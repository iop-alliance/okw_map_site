DevOps at the Internet of Production Alliance

MAP GENERATOR.

 

What: This is a self submission tool for the OKW/mAkE program to survey and collect data on machines, and materials used by locations where manufacturing and innovation happens.

Who: This is intended to be answered by Makerspaces, FabLabs, and innovation hubs, within the OKW/mAkE program scope.

Purpose: The Internet of Production Alliance collects the data to form a database of machines, and materials by location, to be analyzed and mapped for the Open-Know-Where initiative.

Data privacy: The data collected is processed only by the DevOps office. For data privacy concerns and requests contact by email to: antonio.anaya@internetofproduction.org

At The Internet of Production Alliance, we are grateful for the time and effort of your contributions to this data collection exercise.

HOW TO ANSWER THIS FORM.

  1. Add your Facility using the "Add new facility form" below. If your facility is already added on the list continue to add your machines and materials.
  2. Fill out machine data:
    • There is a list of links below, depending on your machine type, select a link, this will redirect you to an additional form that contains data specifically for the type of machine selected. i.e. I want to fill out data for a 3D printer, I click the "ADD 3D printer" link, and fill out the data requested in the form.
  3. Fill out materials data.
    • There's only one form type for materials. 
  4. Fill out the "Feedback form" to provide feedback at any step during the data filling process.
